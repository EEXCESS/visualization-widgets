  • Thank you for taking the time, to help us with this Evaluation.

    This evaluation is about a system that is visualizing recommendations in the area of cultural heritage.

    The idea behind it is the following: You are doing research on a specific topic and you are currently looking at a Wikipedia article. Our recommendation system is analysing the page you are viewing, and is suggesting more documents that may fit to your topic of interest.

    The recommendations are shown as a list of items (e.g. documents or images). The so called "Recommendation Dashboard" (RD) displays these recommendations in a suitable visual form, that helps the user to explore, filter and organize them.

    This evaluation is focused on the Recommendation Dashboard, and therefore you will start directly with a fixed set of recommendations shown in the RD.
