  • Thank you for taking the time, to help us with this Evaluation.

    This evaluation is about a system that is visualizing recommendations in the area of cultural heritage.

    The idea behind is the following: You are doing a research about a specific topic. You start by googling and you will somehow end up in wikipedia. while surfing through the web, our recom;mendation system is analysing what you are interested in, and is suggesting you documents that will fit to your topic of interest.

    The recommendations are typically a List of items/documents/images. The so called "Recommendation Dashboard" is trying to find a better way of representing these recommendations to the user, and helping him to explore, filter and organize them.

    The evaluation is focussed on the Recommendation Dashboard, and therefore you will start with a fixed set of recommendations.
